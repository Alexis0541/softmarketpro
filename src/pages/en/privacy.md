_Last updated_: July 14, 2026

SoftMarket Pro collects the information needed to manage orders, send digital licenses and provide customer support.

## Information the store may request

- First and last name.
- Email address.
- Phone number.
- Country and billing address.
- Purchased products and order details.

## How the information is used

The information is used to process purchases, send licenses, issue invoices, answer requests and improve the service.

## Retention

Order and billing information may be retained for as long as necessary to meet legal and commercial obligations.

## User rights

Customers may request access, correction or deletion of their data through the contact page.

## Security

The store must be connected to a secure payment gateway before processing real payments. Full card details should not be stored on the site.
